Miso Soup

Descripton

Miso soup is the peak of beauty in simplicity and one of the most important dishes in the Japanes culinary canon. It's perfect as an appetizer or as a main course. Miso is always sure to warm your heart.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups dashi: either store bought or homemade will work just fine
  • 4 TBSP white miso paste
  • 1 package silken tofu
  • 1 1/2 tsp wakame seaweed

    • Directions

    1. Dice the tofu into 1/2 inch cubes.
    2. Bring the dashi to a simmer in a soup pot.
    3. Whisk the miso paste until encorporated in the dashi.
    4. Add the tofu and wakame and simmer for 3-5 minutes.
    5. Serve with a scallion garnish!
