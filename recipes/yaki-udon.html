Yaki Udon

Descripton

Yaki udon is classic and simple Japanese dish of chewy noodles with a soy based sause. It can be made with a variety of protiens and vegetables added to make a truly flexible meal. Yaki udon is sure to become a family favorite!

Ingredients

  • 2 packets of precooked udon noodles
  • 2 and a half cups vegetables of choice
  • 1/2 a pound of ground meat
  • 1 TBSP of neutral cooking oil
  • 2 and a half TBSP dark soy sauce
  • 2 TBSP oyster or hoisin sauce
  • 1 TBPS mirin
  • 2 tsp brown sugar
  • 1 tsp rice wine vinegar

Instructions

  1. Combine soy sauce, oyster sauce, mirin, brown sugar, and rice wince vinegar in a bowl and set aside.
  2. Cook udon noodles in oiling water for 1 to 2 minutes.
  3. Strain noodles and rinse in cold water.
  4. Heat you wok and add oil.
  5. Cook ground meat until halfway done.
  6. Add vegetables and contunue cooking until meat is done and vegetables are soft.
  7. Add noodles and sauce cooking for about 3 minutes or until all ingredients are well combined.
  8. Serve immedately!
